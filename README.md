🛒 Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard

📊 Project Overview

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze and visualize retail sales performance across multiple regions, categories, and customer segments.

This project transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights to support decision-making in areas like profit optimization, customer targeting, and inventory planning.


---

🎯 Objective

The main goals of this project are:

Analyze sales, profit, and orders

Identify top-performing products & regions

Detect loss-making segments

Enable data-driven business decisions



---

🧠 Problem Statement

Retail businesses often face:

Difficulty tracking profit vs sales

Lack of visibility into regional performance

Poor understanding of customer segments


This dashboard solves these issues using clear visual storytelling and KPIs.


---

🧰 Tech Stack

Power BI Desktop → Dashboard creation

Power Query → Data cleaning

DAX → Measures & KPIs

Data Modeling → Relationships between tables

File Format → .pbix



---

📂 Dataset Details

The dataset includes:

Orders

Sales

Profit

Quantity

Customer Segment

Region & State

Product Category & Sub-category

Shipping Mode



---

🔧 Data Preparation

Steps performed:

Removed null values

Fixed inconsistent categories

Converted date columns

Created relationships:

Orders ↔ Customers

Orders ↔ Products


Created calculated columns:

Profit Margin

Year, Month

📊 Dashboard Features & Visuals


---

📈 1. Sales Overview (KPI Cards)

Total Sales

Total Profit

Total Orders


💡 Insight:
Quick snapshot of overall business performance.


---

🌍 2. Sales by Region (Map / Bar Chart)

Compare sales across regions


💡 Insight:

West region often generates highest sales

Some regions show high sales but low profit



---

📦 3. Category & Sub-Category Analysis

Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology


💡 Insight:

Technology = highest profit

Furniture = often low or negative profit



---

👥 4. Customer Segment Analysis

Consumer

Corporate

Home Office


💡 Insight:

Consumer segment contributes maximum sales

Corporate segment often more profitable



---

📅 5. Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)

Monthly / Yearly sales trends


💡 Insight:

Seasonal spikes in sales

Growth trends over time



---

🚚 6. Shipping Mode Analysis

Standard Class

Second Class

First Class


💡 Insight:

Standard shipping dominates

Faster shipping increases cost → affects profit



---

💰 7. Profit Analysis

Profit by region, category, and product


💡 Insight:

Some products have high sales but negative profit

Discounts heavily impact profitability



---

📈 Key Insights

✔ High sales ≠ High profit
✔ Technology category drives maximum profit
✔ Furniture category needs optimization
✔ Discounts reduce profitability
✔ Regional performance varies significantly
